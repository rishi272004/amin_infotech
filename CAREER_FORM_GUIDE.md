# Google Form Setup: Amin Infotech Recruitment

Use the following structure and settings to create your official recruitment Google Form. This mirrors the professional "Modern Brand" aesthetic of your website.

---

## Form Settings
- **Theme Color**: #e11d48 (Rose 600)
- **Header Image**: (Recommended: Use your white logo on a dark background)
- **Settings**: 
    - [x] Collect email addresses
    - [x] Send responders a copy of their response
    - [x] Limit to 1 response (recommended)
    - [x] Allow file uploads (Required for Resumes)

---

## Form Structure

### Section 1: Personal Identification
1. **Full Name**
   - Type: Short Answer
   - [Required]

2. **Email Address**
   - Type: Short Answer (Validated for email)
   - [Required]

3. **Contact Number**
   - Type: Short Answer
   - [Required]

4. **LinkedIn Profile / Portfolio Link**
   - Type: Short Answer (URL)
   - [Optional]

---

### Section 2: Professional Background
5. **Position Applied For**
   - Type: Dropdown
   - Options:
     - Tally Implementation Specialist
     - .NET Developer
     - Full Stack Developer (Next.js/React)
     - Sales & Business Development
     - Technical Support Engineer
     - UI/UX Designer
     - Other

6. **Total Years of Experience**
   - Type: Multiple Choice
   - Options:
     - Fresher
     - 1-3 years
     - 3-5 years
     - 5+ years

7. **Key Technical Skills**
   - Type: Checkboxes
   - Options:
     - TallyPrime / Tally.ERP 9
     - C# / .NET
     - JavaScript / TypeScript
     - React / Next.js
     - SQL / Database Management
     - Enterprise Sales
     - Accounting Standards

---

### Section 3: Submissions
8. **Resume/CV Upload**
   - Type: File Upload
   - Supported types: PDF, Word
   - Maximum file size: 10MB
   - [Required]

9. **Briefly describe why you want to join Amin Infotech**
   - Type: Paragraph
   - Description: *What excites you about building the future of FinTech with us?*
   - [Required]

10. **Notice Period (In Days)**
    - Type: Short Answer
    - Description: *Ex: Immediate, 30 Days, 90 Days*

---

## Form Description / Header Text
Copy and paste this into the "Form Description" field at the top:

> **Welcome to the Amin Infotech Strategic Talent Portal.**
> 
> We are looking for visionaries and problem-solvers to redefine how the world handles financial data. Please complete this form to begin your journey with our team.
> 
> All information provided remains confidential and will be used solely for recruitment purposes.
